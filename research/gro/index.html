Research Paper

Title: Optimal Play of the Great Rolled Ones Game

Authors: Todd W. Neller, Phong T. Pham, Quan H. Nguyen, Linh T. Phan, Clifton Presser

Abstract:
In this paper, we solve and visualize optimal play for the Great Rolled Ones jeopardy dice game by Mitschke and Scheunemann. We share the second player advantage and compute that the first player should start with 3 compensation points (komi) for greatest fairness. We present a spectrum of human-playable strategies that trade off greater play complexity for better performance, and collectively clarify key considerations for excellent play.
